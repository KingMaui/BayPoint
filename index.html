How it works

Simple as a tap-in.

01
Pick your course

Search from courses across California — with slope rating and course rating pre-loaded for accurate score differentials.

02
Enter your scorecard

Hole by hole, 9 or 18. Each score color-codes as you type — birdie green, bogey amber, double-bogey red.

03
Watch your index update

Your WHS handicap index recalculates instantly using the best differentials from your round history. No spreadsheets needed.
